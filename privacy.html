Our Commitment to Your Privacy

OneSwitch SA is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly. We collect only the information necessary to assist you with fibre enquiries, service requests, and customer support.

Information We Collect

  • Your full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Installation or service address
  • Details you provide through enquiry forms

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your enquiries.
  • Recommend suitable fibre solutions.
  • Communicate updates about your request.
  • Improve our services and customer experience.

Sharing Your Information

OneSwitch SA does not sell your personal information. Where required to fulfil your request, we may share relevant information with trusted Internet Service Providers, fibre network operators, or installation partners.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, misuse, or disclosure.

Your Rights

You may request access to your personal information, ask for corrections, or request deletion where applicable, subject to South African law.

POPIA Compliance

OneSwitch SA aims to comply with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa and will continue improving its privacy practices as the business grows.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us via our Contact Page.
