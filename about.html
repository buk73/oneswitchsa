Who We Are

OneSwitch SA is a South African connectivity platform focused on making fibre internet access simpler, faster, and more transparent.

We connect customers with suitable fibre solutions by helping them compare options, understand available packages, and make informed switching decisions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify connectivity by creating a trusted bridge between customers and Internet Service Providers.

Our Vision

To become a trusted South African fibre marketplace where customers can easily discover, compare, and connect with the right internet providers.

Our Values

  • Customer First Service
  • Transparency and Trust
  • Reliable Connectivity Solutions
  • Strong ISP Partnerships
  • Innovation Through Technology
