Who We Are

OneSwitch SA is a South African fibre connectivity platform designed to help homes and businesses discover better internet solutions.

We simplify the process of finding fibre by connecting customers with suitable connectivity providers based on their location, requirements and budget.

Our mission is to make choosing fibre easier, more transparent and customer-focused.

Our Mission

Making Connectivity Simple

We remove the confusion from choosing fibre by helping customers understand their options.

Connecting Customers & Providers

We create a bridge between customers searching for fibre and trusted connectivity providers.

Supporting Digital Growth

We believe reliable internet access helps homes, businesses and communities grow.
