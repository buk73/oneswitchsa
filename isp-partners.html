How Our Partnership Process Works

Step 1: Customer Discovery

Customers use OneSwitch SA to compare available connectivity solutions and submit their requirements.

Step 2: Lead Qualification

Customer information is reviewed to understand location, requirements and connectivity needs.

Step 3: Partner Connection

Suitable customer enquiries are introduced to approved partners for follow-up and service delivery.

Step 4: Continuous Growth

We continuously improve our platform to create more opportunities for customers and partners.
